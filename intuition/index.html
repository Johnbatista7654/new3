Introduction

Intuition is the first blockchain built for information finance.

Information is the world’s most valuable resource, driving science, media, governance, trade, AI, and every decision in a $200T+ global knowledge economy. Yet unlike money, it has no native financial infrastructure.

Intuition fills this gap. It’s the settlement and distribution layer for information, making knowledge itself tradable.


Why are we building this?

The global knowledge economy is the world’s largest market. Every trade, financial system, and AI model depends on trusted information, yet knowledge today is fragmented, unverifiable, and monopolized.

Over 90% of the world’s data has been created in just the last two years. AI demand is doubling every 18–24 months, while fewer than 10 companies control more than 80% of global information flows. Individuals generate most of this data, yet capture less than 1% of its economic value.

Blockchains democratized finance by removing gatekeepers and creating open rails. But there’s still no native infrastructure for information, until now.

Intuition is the chain for InfoFi, doing for knowledge what Ethereum did for money. It makes information programmable, composable, and tradable. Facts, claims, and reputations can be verified, staked, and exchanged, turning information into a liquid asset class.

The result: not just more information, but better intuition. A higher-resolution understanding of the world that powers smarter AI, safer markets, and more confident collective decisions.

This unlocks new asset classes: information markets, reputation economies, and context-driven AI, pushing information onto financial rails for the first time.

Introducing TRUST

Every economy needs a currency. For Information Finance (InfoFi), that currency is TRUST — the native token of the Intuition Network.


TRUST powers the world’s knowledge economy, securing the Intuition Network while aligning incentives across builders, communities, and AI systems. More than a token, TRUST is the standard by which all information is denominated — the unit of confidence, reputation, and value.




Token Utility

The TRUST token serves multiple pivotal functions within the Intuition ecosystem:

Gas Token for the Intuition Network

TRUST serves as the native gas token of the Intuition Network. By tying the core operation of the network to the token, demand scales directly with usage.

Data Creation and Curation

Participants use TRUST to create and curate new knowledge on the network. Users can stake TRUST on information to gain a larger share of its ownership and increase its weight.

Incentivizing High-Quality Contributions

The more useful and trusted the information, the more interactions it attracts — and the more TRUST flows back to its contributors.

Bonding for Network Security

Users can bond (akin to staking in Proof of Stake) their TRUST for a set duration, earning protocol emissions in return.

Governance Voting Power

When users bond TRUST they gain voting power, allowing them to both submit new governance proposals and vote on existing ones.
