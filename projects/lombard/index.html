    // Project Summary

    Lombard is building full-stack infrastructure to grow Bitcoin Capital Markets onchain, unlocking the full potential of the most important asset of our generation. Lombard will do for Bitcoin what Tether/Circle have done for stablecoins. 


    Lombard first established market leadership with $LBTC, Bitcoin’s leading yield-bearing asset. Fully backed by BTC and composable across DeFi, $LBTC integrates with protocols such as Aave, Spark, Pendle, Etherfi, and more across 12 blockchains, all while earning yield. It is secured by a decentralized consortium of 14 digital asset institutions and today stands as the largest Bitcoin LST, commanding 57% of the market. $LBTC is also the fourth-largest BTC derivative overall—trailing only non-yield-bearing tokens $WBTC, $CBBTC, and $BTCB.



    Building on this foundation, the Lombard protocol has expanded to provide a full stack of infrastructure, security, and liquidity primitives. These enable platforms like Bybit and Binance to offer Bitcoin staking to their users, chains to launch new Bitcoin-native primitives, and DeFi protocols to onboard BTC assets, users, and liquidity. In short, Bitcoin holders now have a powerful gateway to move capital from cold storage into the onchain economy.


    Lombard is backed by leading institutions, exchanges, and DeFi protocols, and is pioneering the infrastructure for Bitcoin’s capital markets.

    // Key Project Highlights

    • Product Innovation: Introduced $LBTC, the first yield-bearing Bitcoin asset, held by 260K users and available on 12 chains including Ethereum mainnet, Base, Sui, and BNB.

    • 100+ DeFi integrations: $LBTC is trusted collateral across lending, DEXs, restaking, and derivatives protocols including Aave, Pendle, Morpho and Spark.
    • Market Leadership: Established $LBTC as the largest Bitcoin LST, commanding 57% of the market and ranking as the 4th-largest BTC derivative overall.
    • Team Expertise: Built by veterans from Coinbase, Ripple, Deutsche Bank, Microsoft, Maple, Polychain, and Argent, with deep expertise across crypto, finance, and engineering.
    • Backed by the Best: Raised $17M from prominent investors, including Polychain Capital, YZi Labs (formerly Binance Labs), and Franklin Templeton.

    // Innovations & Technology

    Lombard enables BTC holders, institutions, and developers to put Bitcoin to work onchain.


    • $LBTC Launch: On August 21, 2024, Lombard introduced $LBTC, a liquid staked BTC. allowing users to mint LBTC onchain from a Bitcoin network address, and use it across DeFi.
    • Native BTC Yield: On August 11, 2025, $LBTC upgraded to a yield-bearing design, offering native yield of up to 1%—unlocking capital efficiency, passive income, and onchain utility for BTC holders.
    • Best-in-class Security: Lombard employs a trust-minimized and layered security model for BTC that leverages a consortium of some of the largest institutions in crypto as validators including Galaxy, DCG, OKX, Wintermute, Amber, Antpool, F2pool — and involves a set of multiple checks and balances to ensure security.
    • Lombard SDK: A developer-ready toolkit that enables platforms to integrate Native BTC deposits, bridging, and yield generation into wallets, dApps, and financial platforms with ease. Binance and Bybit were among the first adopters, offering $LBTC staking directly in their Web3 and Earn products.
    • Lombard Ledger: Comet BFT consensus layer for secure Bitcoin settlement.
    • Proof-of-Reserves Oracle: $LBTC was the first BTC LST to implement an onchain PoR feed with Redstone Oracles.


    Roadmap: Lombard is developing additional onchain protocols and products at every layer of the stack, including a basis trade vault and a new cross-chain BTC primitive to rival wrapped BTC incumbents. Read more here.
